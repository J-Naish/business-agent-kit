# Streaming TV / Sponsored TV

Use this reference when the plan needs TV-like reach without assuming Amazon DSP access. Amazon has used the **Sponsored TV** name for its self-service streaming TV solution, and some marketplaces now present this through a broader **Streaming TV** campaign flow. Verify the current naming and eligibility in the target account.

## Role

Streaming TV / Sponsored TV is useful when the advertiser needs:

- Upper-funnel reach through full-screen streaming video.
- A self-service path with daily-budget flexibility rather than a traditional TV buy.
- Amazon first-party shopping and streaming signals for awareness or consideration.
- Interactive or shoppable TV experiences where available.
- A bridge into lower-funnel Sponsored Display remarketing where supported.

Do not treat it as a direct replacement for Sponsored Products or Sponsored Brands. It creates demand and memory; it usually needs a separate conversion capture layer.

## How It Differs From Amazon DSP Streaming TV

| Area | Streaming TV / Sponsored TV | Amazon DSP streaming TV |
|---|---|---|
| Access | Self-service in Ads Console or Campaign Manager where available | DSP self-service or managed access |
| Minimum spend | Designed for no-minimum or flexible budget entry in supported markets | Managed service can require larger commitments; self-service access varies |
| Targeting | Simpler modeled or prepackaged audiences | More advanced audience, frequency, supply, and measurement controls |
| Best fit | Small to mid-market TV entry, always-on awareness tests | Larger full-funnel media plans, custom audiences, deeper measurement |

## Planning Guidance

- Use strong product/brand recognition in the first seconds.
- Keep the idea simple enough for a lean-back TV context.
- Plan follow-up capture with Sponsored Display, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Products, or DSP remarketing.
- Measure reach, frequency, video completion, branded search, detail page views, and downstream assisted demand.
- Avoid judging awareness TV only by immediate attributed ROAS.

## Creative Notes

Specs vary by marketplace and campaign flow, but streaming TV creative typically requires high-quality 16:9 video. Verify current file size, duration, bitrate, audio, and interactive asset requirements before production.

## Common Mistakes

- Routing small-budget streaming TV requests directly to DSP without checking self-service Streaming TV / Sponsored TV access.
- Using retail-search copy instead of TV-native storytelling.
- Launching without enough product detail page, Store, or lower-funnel capture readiness.
- Blending streaming TV performance with remarketing and treating the combined ROAS as incremental.
