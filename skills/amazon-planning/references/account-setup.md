# Account Setup, Brand Registry, and Category Eligibility

Use this reference when checking whether an Amazon seller, vendor, or brand is ready to list products and advertise. Requirements vary by marketplace, account type, category, and date. Verify all local documents, fees, and eligibility in the target marketplace before launch.

## Selling Account and Eligibility

| Area | Planning guidance |
|---|---|
| Selling plan | A professional selling plan is usually required for serious marketplace operations, bulk listing tools, business reports, and most advertising use cases. Exact plan names and fees vary by marketplace. |
| Identity verification | Expect business identity, tax, bank, payment, address, and representative verification. Local document names differ by country. |
| Product identifiers | Use valid GTINs such as UPC, EAN, ISBN, or JAN where required. Brand owners may be eligible for GTIN exemption in some categories. |
| Category approval | Some categories, brands, products, and conditions require approval before listing or advertising. Confirm before planning media spend. |
| Featured Offer eligibility | Many Sponsored Products placements require the product to be eligible for the Featured Offer. Pricing, availability, fulfillment, seller performance, and account health affect this. |
| Account health | Poor account health can suppress listings, reduce eligibility, or interrupt ads. Treat health checks as part of media readiness. |

## Account Health Checklist

Monitor the target marketplace's Account Health dashboard and local performance policies. Common signals include:

- Order Defect Rate.
- Cancellation rate.
- Late shipment rate.
- Valid tracking rate.
- Policy violations.
- Product authenticity and safety complaints.
- Intellectual property complaints.
- Response time to customer messages.

Do not rely on one marketplace's threshold as universal. If a metric is near policy limits, fix operations before scaling ads.

## Brand Registry

Amazon Brand Registry is important for brand owners because it unlocks or strengthens:

- Brand protection tools.
- Sponsored Brands and some Sponsored Display features.
- A+ Content and Premium A+ Content where eligible.
- Stores.
- Manage Your Experiments.
- Brand Analytics and Search Query Performance where available.
- Amazon Vine eligibility in supported marketplaces.
- Brand Tailored Promotions in supported marketplaces.
- Amazon Attribution access in supported marketplaces.

Typical requirements include a registered or pending trademark, a matching brand name, and evidence that the brand is permanently affixed to products or packaging. Trademark status, accepted trademark offices, and approval rules vary by marketplace.

## Category and Compliance Readiness

Before listing or advertising, identify whether the product touches regulated or high-risk areas:

| Risk area | Examples |
|---|---|
| Safety and certification | Electronics, batteries, wireless devices, toys, children's products, automotive parts |
| Health and claims | Supplements, cosmetics, medical devices, drugs, therapeutic claims |
| Food and beverage | Ingredient labeling, shelf life, import rules, alcohol |
| Restricted products | Weapons, adult products, surveillance, hazardous materials |
| Brand restrictions | Gated brands, authorized reseller requirements, invoice requirements |
| Condition limits | New, used, refurbished, collectible, renewed, or category-specific condition rules |

Use official Amazon category guidance and local legal counsel for regulated products. Do not promise approval when requirements depend on account history or Amazon review.

## Launch Readiness Gate

Do not recommend aggressive ad spend until these items are credible:

- Listing can be created or edited by the account.
- Product identifiers and variations are valid.
- Category approvals are cleared or the risk is explicitly documented.
- Fulfillment method and inventory coverage are viable.
- Main image and product detail page comply with Amazon policies.
- Pricing is competitive enough to win traffic and, where relevant, the Featured Offer.
- Brand Registry status is known if Sponsored Brands, Stores, A+ Content, or Brand Analytics are part of the plan.
