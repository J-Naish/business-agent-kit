# Video Ads

Video Ads are Promoted Ads that use video to demonstrate, explain, entertain, or build trust.

Spec source: X Creative Ad Specifications (https://business.x.com/en/help/campaign-setup/creative-ad-specifications) and 2026 aspect-ratio announcement (https://business.x.com/en/blog/x-expands-aspect-ratio-support-for-ad-creatives.html). Last verified: 2026-04.

## Specs

| Item | Guidance |
|---|---|
| File type | MP4 or MOV |
| Common aspect ratios | 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 4:5, 2:3 where supported |
| File size | 1 GB max; under 30 MB strongly recommended |
| Recommended short-form length | 15 seconds or less for in-feed Video Ads |
| Maximum length | Up to 2:20 supported; select advertisers may request up to 10 minutes |
| Frame rate | 29.97 or 30 FPS recommended; up to 60 FPS supported |
| Bitrate | 6,000-10,000k for 1080p; 5,000-8,000k for 720p |
| Video codec | H.264 Baseline/Main/High Profile with 4:2:0 color space |
| Audio codec | AAC LC |
| Looping | Videos under 60 seconds can loop |
| Audio | Use captions or text overlays because users may watch muted |

X expanded 4:5 and 2:3 support in 2026 for cross-platform creative reuse. Verify current X creative specifications for exact limits by placement and buying path.

## Best Uses

- Product demos.
- Founder or customer story.
- Before/after or process explanation.
- Event promotion.
- App walkthrough.
- B2B or SaaS education.

## Structure

```text
0-2s: Hook or visual pattern interrupt
2-8s: Product, problem, or proof
8-15s: Benefit and CTA
```

Longer videos can work for education or premium placements, but the first seconds still carry the burden.

## Creative Practices

- Show the product or outcome early.
- Use captions by default.
- Keep branding visible but not intrusive.
- Design for mobile-first viewing.
- Align the CTA with the card or destination.
- Avoid slow logo intros.

## Metrics

| Objective | Primary read |
|---|---|
| Reach | CPM, reach, frequency |
| Video views | View rate, CPV, completion rate |
| Website traffic | CTR, CPC, landing page behavior |
| Website conversions / Sales | CPA, ROAS, conversion quality |

## Common Mistakes

- Opening with a long brand animation.
- Making the video understandable only with sound.
- Optimizing for views when the business needs conversions.
- Reusing a TV-style cut without adapting to the X timeline.

## Diagnosis

| Symptom | Likely cause | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Low view rate | Slow first frame or weak hook | Start with product, outcome, tension, or motion |
| Strong views, weak clicks | CTA appears late or message is awareness-only | Add earlier value/offer cue and align card CTA |
| High CPV | Audience/context mismatch or weak thumbnail/opening | Test different first 2 seconds and targeting signal |

## Operating Cadence

- Read first-day delivery for obvious hook failures, but wait for enough spend before judging CPA/ROAS.
- Replace fatigued openings before rebuilding the whole concept.
- Keep a log of hook, proof, CTA, length, and aspect ratio for each test.
