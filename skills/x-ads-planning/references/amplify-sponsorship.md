# Amplify Sponsorship

Amplify Sponsorship provides a 1:1 pairing with a publisher during a selected moment, with Post-level control for the campaign period.

## Availability

Amplify Sponsorship is generally a managed-service product and may not be available to self-serve advertisers. Confirm availability, inventory, dates, publisher fit, and minimum spend with X.

## Best Uses

- Sports, entertainment, news, cultural events, and tentpole moments.
- Brand awareness and association with premium content.
- Product launches that need high-trust media context.
- Campaigns where exclusivity and alignment matter more than cheap reach.

## Planning Inputs

| Input | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Publisher / content partner | Determines contextual fit and brand safety |
| Moment or flight dates | Sponsorship value depends on timing |
| Creative assets | Must fit video context and brand association |
| Measurement plan | Lift, reach, frequency, view quality, and downstream impact |
| Approval workflow | Publisher and X approvals may affect timelines |

## Creative Guidance

| Element | Guidance |
|---|---|
| File type | MP4 or MOV |
| File size | 1 GB max |
| Length | 6 seconds or less recommended; 2:20 max |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 or 1:1 recommended depending on publisher content |
| Size | 1200x1200 recommended; 600x600 minimum for 1:1 |
| Cropping | If height exceeds width, video can crop to 1:1 in feed |

- Make the brand cue clear early.
- Align tone with the publisher and content moment.
- Avoid generic spots that could run anywhere.
- Use a simple message that can be understood quickly before or around video content.

## Measurement

Use reach, frequency, video view quality, brand lift where available, search/direct lift, and downstream site or conversion signals. Do not judge sponsorships only by last-click CPA.
