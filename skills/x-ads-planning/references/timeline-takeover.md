# Timeline Takeover

Timeline Takeover is a premium X Takeover product that puts a brand's ad at the top of the conversation as the first ad of the day when people open X.

Spec source: X Ad formats (https://business.x.com/en/advertising/formats) and X Takeover guidelines. Last verified: 2026-04.

## Availability

Timeline Takeover is a managed-service premium placement. Confirm availability, market, dates, eligibility, pricing, creative specs, and approval timelines with X before recommending it as final.

## Best Uses

- Major launches.
- Large events.
- Entertainment releases.
- National or global brand moments.
- Campaigns where maximum single-day reach matters.

## Planning Inputs

| Input | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Launch date / flight date | Inventory is time-sensitive |
| Market | Availability and cost vary |
| Creative assets | Must be ready and approved early |
| Landing destination | High reach can expose weak destinations quickly |
| Measurement | Requires reach, brand, and downstream success criteria |

## Creative Guidance

- Use a simple, high-impact message.
- Brand cue and offer/event must be clear immediately.
- Avoid dense copy.
- Match the takeover creative to the rest of the campaign so follow-up ads feel connected.

## Measurement

| KPI | Use |
|---|---|
| Reach and frequency | Primary delivery read |
| CPM | Cost efficiency |
| Engagement and video metrics | Creative resonance |
| Search/direct/site lift | Demand signal |
| Conversion or revenue | Secondary unless the campaign is explicitly direct response |

## Planning and Cost Checks

Takeover pricing and minimums vary by market, date, inventory, and package. Do not hard-code a price. Ask X for:

- Minimum spend, reservation rules, and market/date availability.
- Whether the buy includes supporting Promoted Ads or other placements.
- Creative approval timeline and backup creative requirements.
- Reporting granularity and lift-study availability.

## Diagnosis

| Symptom | Likely cause | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Reach delivered but weak lift | Creative lacks a memorable offer or reason | Strengthen launch message and supporting media |
| Stakeholders judge on CPA | Wrong KPI frame | Set reach/lift/direct/search KPIs upfront |
| Approval delays | Premium creative not submitted early enough | Build approval buffer into the timeline |
