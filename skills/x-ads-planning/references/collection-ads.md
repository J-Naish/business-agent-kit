# Collection Ads

Collection Ads let advertisers showcase a primary hero image with smaller product thumbnails below. They are useful for discovery, browsing, and commerce-oriented campaigns.

## Use Cases

- E-commerce product discovery.
- Product line launches.
- Seasonal collections.
- Multiple landing pages in one ad unit.
- Retail, fashion, beauty, consumer goods, travel, and marketplace offers.

## Structure

| Element | Role |
|---|---|
| Hero image | Main visual hook and brand/product framing |
| Thumbnail images | Up to 6 products, services, or promotions |
| Destinations | Product-specific or category-specific landing pages |
| Copy | Sets the collection's reason to browse |

## Creative Practices

- Use a strong hero image that explains the collection quickly.
- Keep thumbnails visually consistent.
- Use clear product images, not cluttered lifestyle scenes, for thumbnails.
- Match each thumbnail to the most relevant landing page.
- Use product categories or benefits to organize the collection.

## Measurement

| Goal | Read |
|---|---|
| Product discovery | CTR, engagement, product-page views |
| Sales | CPA, ROAS, product-level revenue |
| New product launch | Reach, CTR, assisted conversions |

## Common Mistakes

- Weak hero image.
- Products with inconsistent visual style.
- Dead or mismatched product URLs.
- Promoting low-margin products without margin visibility.
- Treating Collection Ads as a replacement for catalog/feed hygiene.
