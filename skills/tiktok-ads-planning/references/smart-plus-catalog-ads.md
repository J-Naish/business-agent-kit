# Smart+ Catalog Ads

Smart+ Catalog Ads are catalog-driven product ads for website or app sales destinations. TikTok's older Dynamic Showcase Ads / catalog-ad terminology may still appear in help articles or accounts, but current planning should use **Smart+ Catalog Ads** or **Video Shopping Ads with Catalog** where supported.

## Best Uses

- Catalog-based retargeting.
- Large product sets.
- Personalized product recommendations.
- E-commerce campaigns outside TikTok Shop where eligible.
- Website or app commerce that has a usable catalog but is not using TikTok Shop.

## Requirements

- Product catalog connected and approved.
- At least the minimum viable product set required by the current account flow.
- TikTok Pixel and/or Events API configured.
- Product IDs aligned between events and catalog.
- Product feed with accurate titles, prices, availability, images, and URLs.
- Product sets that reflect category, margin, inventory, or campaign goals.

## Campaign Design

| Role | Use |
|---|---|
| Retargeting | Recover viewers, product-page visitors, cart abandoners |
| Prospecting | Use catalog relevance to find new customers where available |
| Cross-sell / upsell | Promote complementary products to existing customers |

## Measurement

Track CPA, ROAS, product-level revenue, contribution margin, new vs returning buyers, and feed/product errors.

## Common Mistakes

- Product ID mismatches.
- Out-of-stock products in the feed.
- Weak product images.
- Too-broad retargeting windows.
- Reporting revenue without margin or returns.
- Calling the format "DPA" or "Dynamic Showcase Ads" in a client-facing plan when the current account UI uses Smart+ Catalog Ads.
