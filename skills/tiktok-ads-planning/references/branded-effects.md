# Branded Effects

Branded Effects are custom TikTok effects, filters, stickers, or interactive experiences that users can apply to their own videos. They are usually managed products and require TikTok production/spec confirmation.

Effect House is TikTok's production environment for effects. Use it or TikTok/partner production workflows depending on the effect complexity and managed access.

## Best Uses

- Product try-on or visualization.
- Playful interaction with a brand asset.
- Hashtag Challenge support.
- Entertainment, beauty, CPG, gaming, sports, and launch moments.

## Effect Types

| Type | Use |
|---|---|
| 2D effects | Simple overlays, stickers, backgrounds |
| 2D Pro / face effects | Makeup, hair, face transformation, try-on |
| 3D effects | Product objects or richer visual interaction |
| Gamified effects | Mini-game or score-based participation |

## Production Checks

- Confirm Effect House / TikTok specs for cover image, icon, sponsorship marks, package size, and device support before production.
- Use Asset Studio or other Effect House tooling where it speeds up simple masks, stickers, or 2D assets.
- Test on real devices; effects can fail because of face tracking, lighting, device performance, or unclear instructions.

## Creative Guidance

- Make the effect fun without needing long instructions.
- Keep brand elements visible but not intrusive.
- Design around user participation, not brand decoration only.
- Pair with creators, Spark Ads, Branded Hashtag Challenge, or In-Feed support.

## Planning Inputs

| Input | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Effect mechanic | Determines production complexity |
| Market and device support | Effects can behave differently across devices |
| Music/sound | Needs proper ad rights |
| Creator seeding | Effects need demonstrations |
| Moderation | UGC can develop unexpected meanings |

## Measurement

Use effect opens, video creations, views, engagement, creator adoption, hashtag activity, sentiment, and brand lift where available.

## Common Mistakes

- Building an effect that is technically impressive but hard to use.
- Over-branding the experience.
- No creator demonstration.
- No paid support to seed usage.
