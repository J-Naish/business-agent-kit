# Branded Hashtag Challenge

Branded Hashtag Challenge is a managed TikTok format that encourages users to create videos around a branded hashtag and participation prompt. Availability and package details require TikTok confirmation.

Treat Branded Hashtag Challenge as active but less central than it once was. For creator crowdsourcing and paid amplification, compare it against Branded Mission before recommending.

## Best Uses

- Brand participation campaigns.
- Entertainment, sports, fashion, beauty, food, music, and youth-culture moments.
- UGC generation.
- Product launches where a simple action can be copied by users.

## Strategy

A challenge needs more than a hashtag. It needs:

- A simple repeatable action.
- Clear reason to participate.
- Creator seeding.
- Music or sound strategy with proper rights.
- Brand safety and moderation plan.
- Paid amplification before and during the campaign.

## Creative Guidance

- Make the challenge easy to understand in seconds.
- Use creators to demonstrate the behavior.
- Keep the hashtag short, memorable, and unambiguous.
- Avoid complex instructions.
- Use Branded Effects where they make participation easier or more fun.

## Measurement

Track reach, video views, number of user videos, engagement, hashtag page activity, creator participation, sentiment, earned media, and downstream brand/search/site lift where available.

## Common Mistakes

- Launching a hashtag without creator seeding.
- Making participation too difficult.
- Treating UGC volume as business impact without quality review.
- Not preparing moderation and response workflows.
