# Spark Ads

Spark Ads are TikTok's native ad format for promoting organic TikTok posts. The post can come from the advertiser's TikTok account or from a creator who grants authorization.

## Why Use Spark Ads

- Preserves native TikTok behavior and social proof.
- Paid engagement can accrue to the original post.
- Useful for creator content, testimonials, product demos, and organic winners.
- Can support multiple objectives depending on account and campaign setup.

## Authorization and Identity

| Source | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Advertiser-owned post | Linked TikTok account or Business Center authorization |
| Creator post | Creator authorization / video code or authorized account workflow |
| TikTok Shop content | Shop, Showcase, affiliate, or Spark authorization rules depending on setup |

Do not assume the caption can be edited after authorization. Confirm final caption, disclosure, product links, and usage rights before launching.

TikTok is phasing out Custom Identity for advertising. For new campaigns, plan around a linked TikTok account, advertiser-owned Business Account, or other authorized account/post unless the current campaign type is explicitly exempt. Business Center and Ads Manager terminology may refer to this as account linking rather than request access.

Spark Ads authorization duration can vary by workflow and account. Confirm whether the creator/owner can grant the needed duration before building a media plan around the post.

## Best Uses

- Boosting organic posts that already show strong engagement or retention.
- Creator ads where trust and authenticity matter.
- TikTok Shop or Showcase promotion where eligible.
- Retargeting users who engaged with organic or paid content.
- Testing creator concepts before larger media spend.

Spark Ads are not automatically better than non-Spark ads. Prioritize Spark when the post has native proof, comments, creator trust, or organic momentum that the paid version can use.

## Creative Selection

Prioritize posts with:

- Strong first 1-3 seconds.
- Positive comments and credible social proof.
- Natural product integration.
- Clear audience/problem fit.
- Rights cleared for paid usage and target markets.

## Creator Evaluation

Look beyond follower count:

| Signal | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Retention / completion | Better attention signal than audience size |
| Comment quality | Reveals trust, objections, and fit |
| Category fluency | Creator speaks the audience's language |
| Natural product use | Reduces ad-detection and improves believability |
| Production speed | Supports refresh cadence |
| Rights reliability | Avoids launch delays |

## Measurement

Track:

- Paid performance: CPM, CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS, CVR.
- Organic impact: profile visits, followers, comments, shares.
- Creator/content learnings: hook, format, proof style, audience response.

## Common Mistakes

- Boosting posts only because they look polished.
- Using creator content without clear usage rights.
- Forgetting disclosure or regulated-claim review.
- Treating Spark Ads as automatically better than non-Spark creative.
- Using a creator as a billboard instead of letting them tell a credible story.
