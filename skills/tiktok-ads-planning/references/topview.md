# TopView

TopView is a premium TikTok format designed for high-impact video exposure near the start of the user session. Availability, specs, pricing, targeting controls, and buying path depend on market and TikTok managed access.

TikTok's 2026 NewFronts materials also package TopView with Top Feed as **TopReach** for maximum one-day reach. Confirm whether the plan should buy TopView alone, Top Feed, or TopReach.

## Best Uses

- Major brand launches.
- Product or entertainment releases.
- National or regional awareness bursts.
- Campaigns that need high attention and predictable reach.

## Creative Guidance

- Make the first seconds visually decisive.
- Show the brand/product early.
- Use one simple message.
- Build for sound-on viewing but include captions or visual clarity.
- Avoid complex explanations; follow up with In-Feed/Spark retargeting if education is needed.

## Planning Inputs

| Input | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Market/date | Inventory and pricing are reservation-dependent |
| Creative approval timeline | Premium placements need review |
| Follow-up plan | High reach needs retargeting or conversion path |
| Measurement | Brand lift and proxy demand are often more appropriate than CPA |

## Measurement

Use reach, frequency, CPM, video view quality, engagement, brand lift, search/direct lift, profile visits, site visits, and downstream conversion support.

## Common Mistakes

- Judging only by last-click CPA.
- Opening with a slow logo animation.
- Buying premium reach without a follow-up funnel.
- Hard-coding cost estimates without a current TikTok quote.
