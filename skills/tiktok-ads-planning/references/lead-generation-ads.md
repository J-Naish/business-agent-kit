# Lead Generation

TikTok Lead Generation campaigns collect prospect information through TikTok Instant Forms or supported website destinations. Use them when the business can follow up quickly and judge lead quality.

## Best Uses

- B2C service inquiries.
- Education, real estate, finance, events, appointments, and demos where policy allows.
- B2B content offers or consultation requests.
- Creator-style proof that drives form completion.

## Form Strategy

| Form type | Use when |
|---|---|
| More Volume | The offer is simple and lead quality risk is low; this is the default form type in current TikTok Instant Form guidance |
| Higher Intent | Sales follow-up capacity is limited or lead quality matters; adds review/CAPTCHA friction where available |
| Custom questions | You need qualification before CRM follow-up |
| Website form | You need full landing-page context or complex compliance language |

Published Instant Form content generally cannot be edited directly. If questions or text must change, create or attach a new form and check CRM field mapping. TikTok Ads Manager stores lead data for a limited window, so connect Leads Center or CRM before scale.

## Operating Guidance

- Define qualified lead before launch.
- Connect CRM or Leads Center before increasing budget.
- Set a speed-to-lead SLA; slow follow-up can make CPL look efficient while revenue is weak.
- Use qualifying questions only when they improve sales economics enough to justify the friction.
- Daypart or adjust staffing if leads arrive when nobody can respond.
- Track lead stage movement, not only form submits.

## Creative Guidance

- Explain the value of submitting the form.
- Qualify the audience in the hook and body.
- Show proof, examples, or outcomes before asking for information.
- Keep the CTA aligned with the follow-up promise.

## Measurement

Track:

- CPL and form completion rate.
- Qualified lead rate.
- Contact rate and speed-to-lead.
- Sales acceptance, pipeline, and downstream revenue.

## Diagnosis

| Symptom | Likely cause | Action |
|---|---|---|
| CPL low, close rate low | Form too easy or broad promise | Test Higher Intent, qualify in creative, add question |
| CPL high, quality good | Too much friction or narrow audience | Test More Volume or broaden safely |
| Opens high, submits low | Form too long or offer unclear | Reduce fields, clarify proof and CTA |
| Leads good, contact low | CRM/speed issue | Fix routing, SLA, phone/email field quality |

## Common Mistakes

- Optimizing only to cheap leads.
- Asking too many questions without enough value.
- Weak CRM integration or slow follow-up.
- Replacing forms mid-flight without checking delivery and CRM impact.
- Running regulated lead categories without policy review.
