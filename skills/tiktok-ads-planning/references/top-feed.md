# Top Feed

Top Feed is a premium in-feed placement option associated with Reach & Frequency buying. It gives advertisers high-priority in-feed exposure while preserving a feed-like ad experience.

TikTok's 2026 NewFronts materials also package Top Feed with TopView as **TopReach** for maximum one-day reach. Treat Top Feed alone vs TopReach as a managed buying choice.

## Best Uses

- Brand launches.
- Product announcements.
- Upper-funnel video storytelling.
- Campaigns that need premium in-feed position but not a full takeover.

## Planning Requirements

- Confirm current availability and buying path with TikTok.
- Plan as a reservation/premium placement, not as a normal auction ad.
- Prepare creative and approval timelines early.
- Pair with auction campaigns for retargeting or conversion follow-up.

## Creative Guidance

- Use a strong opening in the first seconds.
- Make the brand/product role clear early.
- Keep the video native to the feed.
- Use captions/text overlays and licensed audio.
- Avoid dense brand-film pacing.

## Measurement

Use reach, frequency, video views, view quality, engagement, brand lift where available, site/search lift, and downstream retargeting performance.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating Top Feed as a direct-response-only placement.
- Using a generic brand film with a slow opening.
- Not preparing follow-up auction campaigns.
- Ignoring approval deadlines.
