# Video Shopping Ads

Video Shopping Ads are shoppable video ads for eligible commerce setups. Current TikTok Help Center guidance says GMV Max is the default and only supported campaign type for new TikTok Shop Ads from July 2025. Video Shopping Ads can still appear in Showcase, catalog, or legacy/eligible contexts. Verify current account availability before recommending.

## Current Path Split

| Product source / destination | Planning posture |
|---|---|
| TikTok Shop Sales | Plan Product GMV Max or LIVE GMV Max by default |
| Showcase | Video Shopping Ads may still be available |
| Non-Shop catalog / website or app commerce | Use Smart+ Catalog Ads or VSA with Catalog where the account supports it |
| Existing legacy campaigns | May remain active; do not assume edit/duplicate/create permissions |

## Best Uses

- Shoppable videos where a product can be demonstrated quickly.
- Creator or Spark-style product proof.
- Showcase-based commerce where eligible.
- Retargeting users who engaged with product content.

## Planning Requirements

| Requirement | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Product source | TikTok Shop, Showcase, or eligible catalog/source determines availability |
| Product permissions | Ads account must have access to the shop/showcase/product source |
| Creative | Video must clearly show product use, benefit, and CTA |
| Measurement | Use TikTok Shop/Seller Center or external commerce reporting as source of truth |

## Creative Guidance

- Show the product in the first seconds.
- Use creator-style demonstration and social proof.
- Mention the key buying reason: price, feature, bundle, use case, result, or availability.
- Keep product claims compliant and substantiated.
- Match the product link/destination to the video.
- Use comments, reviews, and creator proof to answer product objections.

## Measurement

Track product clicks, orders, GMV, ROAS/ROI, new buyers, repeat buyers, affiliate commission impact, and product-level margin where available.

Reconcile attributed GMV to net revenue after discounts, fees, returns, affiliate commissions, and fulfillment. Do not treat platform GMV as payable profit.

## Common Mistakes

- Planning new Video Shopping Ads for TikTok Shop when the account must use GMV Max.
- Using product videos without clear product links or permissions.
- Reporting only attributed GMV without margin or incrementality.
- Ignoring comments, returns, and fulfillment quality.
