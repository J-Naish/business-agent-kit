# NewFronts 2026 Premium Formats

TikTok announced several high-impact brand solutions at NewFronts 2026. Treat these as managed / premium products until the account, market, pricing, and buying path are confirmed by TikTok.

## Formats

| Format | Use |
|---|---|
| Logo Takeover | Co-branded app-open exposure for a major launch or cultural moment |
| Prime Time | Sequential storytelling: up to three ads from one advertiser to the same user within a short time window |
| TopReach | Bundled TopView + Top Feed buy for maximum one-day reach |
| Pulse Mentions | Place ads adjacent to real-time conversations about the brand or category |
| Pulse Tastemakers | Align ads with a selected set of eligible creators through the Pulse suite |

## Planning Guidance

- Use these for major awareness, tentpole, entertainment, launch, or cultural-moment campaigns.
- Confirm market availability, package requirements, creative specs, lead times, exclusivity, and measurement options with TikTok.
- Plan follow-up auction campaigns for retargeting, Search Ads, Spark Ads, website conversion, Shop, or lead capture.
- Use brand lift, reach/frequency, view quality, search/direct lift, and downstream pool growth rather than last-click CPA as the primary readout.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating newly announced premium formats as self-serve products.
- Buying a high-impact surface without a follow-up funnel.
- Using broad brand-film creative instead of one sharp message for the moment.
- Hard-coding pricing or availability from another market.
