# GMV Max

GMV Max is TikTok's automated campaign type for TikTok Shop sales. Current TikTok Help Center guidance says GMV Max is the default and only supported campaign type for new TikTok Shop Ads from July 2025.

## Types

| Type | Use |
|---|---|
| Product GMV Max | Optimize product sales using eligible products, creative assets, and placements |
| LIVE GMV Max | Optimize LIVE room traffic and revenue for TikTok LIVE selling |

Verify LIVE GMV Max country and account eligibility before recommending it. LIVE commerce availability and setup paths vary more than Product GMV Max.

## Best Uses

- TikTok Shop sellers with enough product, order, and creative signal.
- Always-on shop sales.
- Product launches where TikTok Shop is the primary purchase destination.
- LIVE commerce with a real run-of-show and inventory plan.

## Product Readiness

GMV Max performance depends on commerce operations:

- Product listing quality.
- Price and offer competitiveness.
- Reviews and ratings.
- Inventory and fulfillment reliability.
- Return/refund experience.
- Product margin after fees, discounts, shipping, and affiliate commissions.
- Creator, affiliate, Spark, and product-video supply.

## Requirements

- TikTok Shop connected to the relevant Business Center / ad account.
- Correct GMV Max authorization for the shop.
- Product listings, prices, inventory, fulfillment, and shop operations ready.
- Creative assets available from official, business, authorized, Spark Ads, affiliate, or other eligible sources.
- ROI target and budget aligned with business economics.

## Operating Guidance

- Treat GMV Max as a commerce system, not just an ad campaign.
- Validate TikTok-reported GMV against Seller Center, finance, fulfillment, returns, and margin.
- Provide fresh product videos and creator content; automation cannot compensate for weak product creative.
- Monitor product-level outcomes where available.
- Be careful when multiple teams or ad accounts need access; GMV Max authorization can be tied to the shop's primary ad account.
- Check whether **ROI Protection** applies. TikTok guidance says ROI Protection supports GMV Max campaigns created through Seller Center and TikTok Ads Manager from February 25, 2026, and can issue ad credits when eligible campaigns fall below the protected ROI threshold.
- Check whether **auto budget increase** is enabled. TikTok can automatically increase daily budget when ROI and budget utilization are strong, then reset the campaign budget the next day.

## Optimization Posture

| Situation | Posture |
|---|---|
| New product / sparse order history | Consider exploratory delivery first, then tighten Target ROI after signal |
| Stable seller economics | Use Target ROI aligned to contribution margin, not vanity ROAS |
| Limited-time sale or launch | Volume posture can make sense, but reconcile margin quickly |
| Weak creative pool | Add creator/affiliate/product videos before changing targets |
| Profit weak despite good GMV | Inspect returns, fees, discounts, commission, product mix, new buyer rate |

Treat aggressive Target ROI changes as material edits. Check current ROI Protection rules before changing targets.

## LIVE Commerce Notes

LIVE GMV Max needs operations, not just media:

- Host, offer, inventory, and run-of-show must be ready.
- Welcome new viewers repeatedly because room composition changes.
- Repeat core offers during the session.
- Use short videos and Spark-style proof to drive traffic into the LIVE room where eligible.
- Monitor product-level and session-level economics, not only room traffic.

## Watch-Outs

- Less manual control than traditional campaign structures.
- Reporting may not answer every broader media strategy question.
- ROI target settings can restrict scale if unrealistic.
- Manual ROI target changes can affect ROI Protection eligibility.
- Large brands may need additional incrementality and attribution validation.
- GMV can include demand that is not fully incremental. Validate against finance and, where possible, holdouts or lift tests.
